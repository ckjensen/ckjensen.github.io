Giclee Prints
Giclee Fine Art Prints - The papers and inks are not only archival but use some of the most accurate print technology for full color prints. Remember, that the giclee printing process is essentially an inkjet process in which archival grade inks are sprayed onto the surface of the paper. This allows for unrivaled color accuracy and consistency. Even with the advantage the modern printing methods offer, the paper you select can make a difference in how the image appears.To extend the life-span, store your giclee fine art paper print out of direct sunlight and avoid extreme temperature changes or exposure to humidity. It is recommended that paper prints be framed behind glass or acrylic glazing when ultimately on display. While it remains unframed you should keep the prints sealed in clear bags which are acid free like what we offer for sale on our website.
